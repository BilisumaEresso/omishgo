ENVIRONMENT CONFIGURATION

Development Environment (.env):
  NODE_ENV=development
  Result: Device lock DISABLED, multiple devices allowed

Production Environment (.env):
  NODE_ENV=production
  Result: Device lock ENABLED, one device per account

This is the ONLY setting that controls development vs production behavior.
